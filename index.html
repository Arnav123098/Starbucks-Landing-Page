Welcome to Starbucks!

Starbucks sells hot and cold drinks, coffee beans, salads, hot and cold sandwiches, sweet pastries,
snacks, and items such as glasses and tumblers. Through its Starbucks Entertainment division and the
Hear Music brand, the company also markets books, music and films. Many of the company's products
are seasonal or specific to the area where the shop is located. Starbucks ice cream and coffee
are also sold in the grocery store.
